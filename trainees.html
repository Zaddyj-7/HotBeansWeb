Trainee Profiles

Daniel Lecroy

I'm Daniel and probably not the typical designer positioned behind an Illustrator artboard adjusting pixels, but I design. Immersed in stylesheets tweaking font sizes and contemplating layouts is where you'll find me.

I am committed to creating fluent user experiences while staying fashionable. In building JavaScript applications, I use the right tools to deliver fast, resilient solutions optimised for scale.

I draw inspiration from many places when designing websites, including CSS Zen Garden and cultural art from around the world.

Li Chang

Hello! My name is Li and I enjoy creating things that live on the internet.

I chose this career because I grew up with a strong influence from my father, who ran his own software house. Watching him use code to communicate with computers inspired my interest in technology.

I got my job with Hot Beans Web during my final year of university. I applied for a front-end engineer role and spoke about my interest in design, development and web accessibility during the interview.

As a graduate accessibility specialist, I work with designers and developers to build new features and carry out accessibility audits against WCAG guidelines. This helps make sure our websites can be used by everyone, regardless of disability.

The skills needed for my role include determination to learn, good communication, analytical skills, adaptability and collaboration.

The part I enjoy most is working with inspiring developers and designers while helping make websites accessible and safe for everyone.

Rosa DeCastello

Hi, I'm Rosa and I have a real passion for creative design. I'm never happier than when I'm pushing what can be done with HTML and CSS.

I came into web design from an art background but soon realised I needed to develop my technical skills as well. I completed online courses and used support from colleagues to improve my skills.

My work with Hot Beans Web is varied, and I enjoy the challenge of not knowing what industry our next client will come from. It keeps my creativity fresh.

On a day-to-day basis I help maintain and develop client websites, attend meetings, use content management systems, analyse website statistics and carry out quality assurance checks.

The part I enjoy most is being both technical and creative. It is the perfect mix.
