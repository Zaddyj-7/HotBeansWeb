Privacy Policy

This privacy policy is for this website.

Policy Key Definitions

"I", "our", "us", or "we" refer to the business, Hot Beans Web.

"You" or "the user" refers to the person using this website.

GDPR means General Data Protection Regulation. PECR means Privacy and Electronic Communications Regulation. Cookies are small files stored on a user's computer or device.

Processing of Your Personal Data

Under the GDPR, we control and process any personal information you provide to us electronically. Unless otherwise defined, we only process your personal data for accounts and records, advertising, marketing, public relations or staff administration purposes where applicable.

We use the contract lawful basis under the GDPR for processing your information to provide you with details about our services, supplying a quote or estimate.

We shall continue to process your information until the contract between us ends or is terminated under any contract terms. Your personal data under the contract lawful basis is not shared with third parties.

Application Form Data We Collect

When users complete the online application form, Hot Beans Web may collect the following information:

  • Full name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Position applied for
  • Highest qualification
  • Uploaded CV
  • Application message or personal statement
  • IP address

Consent and Marketing

We use the consent lawful basis under the GDPR for processing your information when you explicitly consent to subscribe to our email mailing list. Your information is used to send marketing messages within the scope outlined at the point of subscription.

You can unsubscribe or manage your personal information at any time through links provided in any subscription confirmation email or marketing message.

Legitimate Interests

We use the legitimate interests lawful basis under the GDPR for processing your information where a shared interest exists between us. Your information is not shared with third parties and is only stored for as long as a legitimate interest exists.

Data Security and Protection

Any personal information processed is done securely with protective measures in place to prevent data breaches. This includes information saved to computers, mobile devices, phones, laptops or tablets.

Protective measures include passwords, pass codes, fingerprint recognition software and firewall technology. Our methods meet GDPR compliance requirements.

Your Individual Rights

Under the GDPR your rights are as follows:

  • The right to be informed
  • The right of access
  • The right to rectification
  • The right to erasure
  • The right to restrict processing
  • The right to data portability
  • The right to object
  • The right not to be subject to automated decision-making including profiling

You also have the right to complain to the ICO if you feel there is a problem with the way we are handling your data.

Internet Cookies

We use cookies on this website to provide users with a better experience. Cookies may be used to track how users use the website, record whether users have seen particular messages, keep users logged in where applicable, display relevant adverts or content, or refer users to a third-party website.

Some cookies are required to enjoy and use the full functionality of this website. Users can manage and delete cookies through their web browser settings.

Cookies that may be used include Google Universal Analytics cookies such as _ga, _gat and _gid.

Transparent Privacy Explanations

We have provided these explanations about user privacy and how this website is used to promote a transparent and honest privacy approach.
