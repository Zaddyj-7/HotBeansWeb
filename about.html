About Hot Beans Web

Company Profile

Hot Beans Web was established in 2007 by David Wood, who previously held roles in a multi-national business. He saw that there was potential for a slightly different type of web design agency.

David wanted to create a web design agency that could deliver good quality websites at affordable prices without compromising on quality.

Over a decade and a half later, this has proven to be a successful formula. Hot Beans Web has grown year on year for both the business and its clients. The company has its head office in Peterborough and offices in Northampton.

Our Trusting Clients

Our client base includes many different types and sizes of business, ranging from sole traders to SMEs and household names.

Hot Beans Web has had direct and indirect development contracts with some of the world's biggest names, including Belkin, DSG Group, Ingram Micro, Halfords, Nationwide, and Tesco.

However, our favourite customers are those who embrace a website to help start and develop their businesses. These include local plumbers, builders, electricians, home builders and gardeners.

We are always here to help and believe in Givers Gain. If you need any help or advice, we are here to help, and we do not charge for consultation.

Our People

We are proud of the people who make Hot Beans Web who we are. They are a unique set of web designers, web developers and marketers who work together to deliver one shared philosophy: to provide the best solutions for website design to existing and new customers.

The team at Hot Beans Web is unusually diverse for the web design industry. As a team, we can focus more on our clients' objectives and deliver custom web design and development that exceeds customer expectations.
